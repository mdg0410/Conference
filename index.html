Featured Speakers

Yomico Moreno

Yomico Moreno is an acclaimed tattoo artist based in Venezuela who is known for his surreal and hyperreal style.

His artwork has earned him incredible recognition in both the international and Latin American tattoo community.

Doctor Woo

Known as Dr. Woo, he is undoubtedly one of the best tattoo artists for Hollywood stars.

He has been tattooing since the age of 13 and perfected his technique as a black and gray tattooist with a specialty in fine single needle lines at Shamrock Social Club, Los Angeles.
